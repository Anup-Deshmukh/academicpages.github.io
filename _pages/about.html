My recent work [_"A Scalable Clustering Algorithm for Serendipity in Recommender Systems"_](https://anup-deshmukh.github.io/publication/2009-10-01-paper-title-number-1) is accepted in the Recommender Systmes track of [ICDM 2018](http://icdm2018.org/).
