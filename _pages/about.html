Hello! I'm Anup, I currently reside in Bangalore where I'm majoring in computer science engineering at IIIT-B (final year). I am part of the Multimodal Perception lab, mentored by Prof. Dinesh Babu. Previously, I was part of FAST lab at CentraleSupelec, France in the summer of 2018 (details here).

I enjoy working on Computer Vision and Deep Learning methods; and my research interests include related fields such as Human-Computer interaction and Recommender Systems.


My recent work "A Scalable Clustering Algorithm for Serendipity in Recommender Systems" is accepted in the Recommender Systmes track of ICDM 2018. I am currently working on formalizing the minimax game in GAN's to give unexpected yet relevant recommendations. The current work aims at exploiting the use of Imitation learning in giving rewards to Generator for it's discrete steps.
